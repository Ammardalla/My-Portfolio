I am an Informatics Engineer with more than 10 years of experience in the technology field, 2 years of teaching computer engineering courses at universities and private institutes, and over 3 years of specialized expertise in developing websites and applications. Throughout my career, I have built comprehensive digital solutions that combine high performance, security, and ease of use. My passion lies in transforming ideas into real products that drive business growth and create meaningful connections between brands and their audiences.
I believe that a website is not just lines of code, but a complete experience through which users form their first impression of a project. For this reason, I ensure that every website I develop is fast, fully responsive across all devices, and optimized for search engines (SEO) to reach the widest possible audience.
A core value I uphold is accessibility, making websites inclusive for people with disabilities, such as those who rely on screen readers. I view this not only as a professional standard but also as a human responsibility that every developer should embrace to contribute to a more fair and inclusive web.
I thrive under pressure without compromising quality, and I am committed to meeting deadlines with precision and attention to detail. At the same time, I continuously keep pace with the latest global trends and technologies in web development, enabling me to deliver innovative and effective solutions that align with evolving market needs.
My ultimate goal is to ensure that the projects I work on go beyond being just websites or applications; I strive to craft complete digital experiences that leave a lasting impact and actively contribute to the success and growth of their owners.
